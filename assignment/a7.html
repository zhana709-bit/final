Assignment 7 Autobiographical Game

This autobiographical game is based on something I experience every morning: waking up, feeding Eevee, and eventually playing with her. These moments are usually so familiar that I barely think about them, but when I had to break them down into scenes and gameplay mechanics, I ended up observing my daily routine much more closely. Turning something ordinary into an interactive experience felt special and surprisingly enjoyable.
