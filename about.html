About Me

Hi, I’m Ada Zhang. I work with illustration and interaction, and I am currently an interaction student with an illustration background.

My work often starts from small, ordinary moments in daily life. I am interested in turning tiny experiences, routines, and emotions into visual or interactive forms. Through drawing, small games, and interaction experiments, I try to look closer at these moments and think about how design can respond to them.

This website collects my course projects and experiments from this semester. It reflects my learning process, including trying, adjusting, and continuing to explore.
